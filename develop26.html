EMERGENCY THINKING DUCK

This duck can help you think!

If you're thinking through a difficult problem, rubberducking is unreasonably effective.

People are increasingly turning to LLMs instead, but getting an answer can backfire.

At best you work through that instead, and it delays you getting back to your own thoughts.

At worst it's tempting to just take that answer at face value (or not even read it!).

Don't let your rubber duck do the work for you!

But if you want help thinking, and not answers from an LLM, contact me!

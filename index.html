When we can help

GROWTH

  • IF: Growth is putting pressure on culture, and your processes and comms are struggling with scale.
  • THEN: We help redesign your operating system in a way that's consistent with your culture.

MAJOR PIVOT

  • IF: You need everyone onboard with a restructure, M&A integration, or other big strategic change.
  • THEN: We help identify and solve for resistance to change, maximising team alignment.

LEADERSHIP TRANSITION

  • IF: Your leadership structure is changing; hiring new execs, making promotions, or stepping back.
  • THEN: We help design process and comms changes that give new leaders the best chance of success.

CONFLICT

  • IF: You have a leadership team in conflict, cross-functional tensions, or recurrent toxicity.
  • THEN: We help identify the core issue so you can make decisive progress, and use conflict more productively.

CRISIS

  • IF: You're missing deadlines, have high turnover, or have suffered a project failure.
  • THEN: We help you think clearly about next steps under high pressure and low mood.

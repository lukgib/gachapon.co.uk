When we can help

  • GROWTH
    • Scale puts pressure on culture, as you leave behind stable sweet-spot sizes where things just work.
    • We can help you scale operations in a way that stays consistent with your culture.
  • MAJOR PIVOT
    • eg. M&A integration, restructuring, new strategy or product, big process changes
    • We identify and solve for resistance to change, in the moments when you need everyone onboard.
  • LEADERSHIP TRANSITION
    • eg. Exec hires, internal promotions, board changes, founders stepping back
    • We can help you make the required org changes to give new leaders the best chance of success.
  • CONFLICT
    • eg. Exec disagreements, cross functional tensions, creative vs commercial
    • We can help teams turn destructive conflict into generative tension.
  • CRISIS
    • eg. Missed deadlines, project failures, bad press, high turnover
    • We can help restore clear thinking under high pressure and low mood.
